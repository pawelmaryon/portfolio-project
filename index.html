About me

Hello, my name is Pawel, a couple of years ago I have discovered the beauty of web development, I've started off with building simple HTML and CSS websites.

All the self-study was fun, but in 2020 I decided to invest my money and time to go that extra mile, so I've joined the LeWagon coding Bootcamp based in London. Nine weeks of intensive study made a huge positive impact on my skill set and web development knowledge. Working with technologies like Ruby, Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL, PostgreSQL, JSON, Figma. MVC software design pattern showed me a completely different approach to web development and how important parts of creating and maintaining online apps are front-end, back-end, and database.

For two years after completing my first coding school I have built apps for friends and family, you will find them in this portfolio. Coding proved to be addictive, so I have invested in another 12 weeks of coding Bootcamp JustIT, where I got to know new technologies like Python, MongoDB, MySQL, Flask, and improve JavaScript. Now I feel like my passion could become my full-time job, so I'm on the hunt for a junior dev position to finally start my career and learn among professionals.
